Grits

Hearty grits to start your day!

Ingredients

    Grits
    Water
    Salt & Pepper
    butter

Cooking Steps

    Wash grits
    Add water to pot with grits and cook for about 10 minutes until bubbling
    Reduce heat and season to taste with salt & pepper
    Cook until grits is nice and smooth
    Remove from heat and add butter to taste
    Enjoy!
