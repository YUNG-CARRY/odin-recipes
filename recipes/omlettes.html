Omlettes

This is what you call a nice seasoned omlette!

Ingredients

    Eggs
    Bacon
    Chopped Onion
    Mushrooms
    Diced Tomatoes
    Diced Peppers
    Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Cooking Steps

    Cook the bacon
    Scramble the egg with salt and pepper to taste
    Remove bacon and chop/shred
    Cook vegetables in remaining bacon grease.
    Cook egg with butter until it is slightly done
    Add chopped/shredded bacon and vegetables to one side of the omlette
    Fold over the ingredients
    Cook until done and serve
